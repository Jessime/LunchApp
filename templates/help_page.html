I'm the Lunch Lady. It's my job to get your group to lunch.

Description

We want to fairly and quickly make a group decision about where to go to lunch.
That means:
  1. Everyone get's an equal say in where the group goes.
  2. You shouldn't have to click more than a couple buttons a week to make a decision.

Basics

The home screen consists of two buttons, 'CHECK IN' and 'PICK LUNCH'. These two buttons are the only ones that have to be clicked before deciding where to eat.

CHECK IN

Because the group is made up of different people each week, we don't want people who aren't present to count in the vote. Make sure everyone is checked in before picking the restaurant.

PICK LUNCH

The app picks a lunch spot through a simple three-step process:
  1. It filters all of the group members down to the ones who are currently checked in.
  2. It finds the probability of going to each restaurant, based on how people spent their 10 points.
  3. The restaurant is randomly chosen based on these probabilities.
This means that if the group decides they really don't want to go to that spot (maybe you ate there last week), just click 'PICK LUNCH' again. You'll probably get a different answer.
